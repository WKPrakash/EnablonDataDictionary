Crisis
Intoduction

Crisis are created and deployed by Central Administrators. Local Administrators can also create and deploy Crisiss, but only within their scope. Crisis Manager is used to assist in:

  • Meeting collection and reporting requirements
  • Evaluating Crisiss according to objectives and internal controls
  • Managing Crisiss
  • Creating, editing and closing Crisiss

    • Crisiss are launched in two stages:

  • Creation: define the Crisis scope and collection period.
  • Launch: create evaluations.
