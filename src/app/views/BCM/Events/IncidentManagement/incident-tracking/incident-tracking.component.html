Incident Tracking
Intoduction

Incident Tracking are created and deployed by Central Administrators. Local Administrators can also create and deploy Incidents, but only within their scope. Incident Manager is used to assist in:

  • Meeting collection and reporting requirements
  • Evaluating Incidents according to objectives and internal controls
  • Managing Incidents
  • Creating, editing and closing Incidents

    • Incident Tracking are launched in two stages:

  • Creation: define the Incident scope and collection period.
  • Launch: create evaluations.
