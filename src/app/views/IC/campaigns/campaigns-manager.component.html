Campaigns Manager
Intoduction

Campaigns are created and deployed by Central Administrators. Local Administrators can also create and deploy campaigns, but only within their scope. Campaign Manager is used to assist in:

  • Meeting collection and reporting requirements
  • Evaluating campaigns according to objectives and internal controls
  • Managing campaigns
  • Creating, editing and closing campaigns

    • Campaigns are launched in two stages:

  • Creation: define the campaign scope and collection period.
  • Launch: create evaluations.
